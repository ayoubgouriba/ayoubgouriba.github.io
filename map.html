Forest Types & Characteristics in Lawrencetown, NS:
Hemlock, Black and White Spruce

In this cartography project, I explored the diverse forest types and characteristics found in Lawrencetown, Nova Scotia. By utilizing forestry data from Nova Scotia Geographic Database , I mapped the distribution of Hemlock, Black Spruce, and White Spruce forests in the area. The map showcases the age and crown closure percentages of each forest type, providing an insightful overview of the local forest landscape. Additionally, the project highlights points of interest, roads, and land cover features, resulting in a comprehensive visualization of the region.
