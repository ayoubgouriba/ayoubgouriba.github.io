Estimating Solar Energy Potential in Ottawa's Sandy Hill Neighborhood using ArcGIS Pro

Link to the poster (pdf) available here.

This project estimated the solar power potential of Ottawa's Sandy Hill neighborhood using ArcGIS Pro. Spatial analysis tools were used to calculate the amount of solar radiation received by each suitable rooftop, identifying areas with the highest potential for energy production. Further details on the methodology and findings can be found in the accompanying PDF document linked above. Additionally, a visualization of the project results can be found in Fig. 1 below. This project showcases the potential of GIS technology in sustainable urban planning and renewable energy assessment.
